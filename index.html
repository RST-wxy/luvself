My Journey

Wounded healer, published author, spiritual companion

From Healthcare to Heart-Care

For decades as an RN and social worker, I witnessed the incredible resilience of the human spirit. Now retired from clinical practice, I offer something different: spiritual companionship beyond diagnosis, beyond treatment plans.

As a Stage IV Breast Cancer Thriver, I understand facing mortality not as an end, but as an invitation to live more authentically. My memoir "Pinto Beans and Cornbread Kept Us All Fed" chronicles finding nourishment in unexpected places.

For over 40 years as a recovery sponsor and lifetime student of A Course in Miracles, I've walked alongside others in their darkest hours, learning that healing isn't about fixing what's broken, but discovering what was whole all along.
